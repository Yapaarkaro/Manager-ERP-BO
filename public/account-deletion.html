Delete your account

You can delete your account directly from within the Manager app:

  1. Open the Manager app
  2. Go to Settings (bottom navigation)
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your decision
  5. Your account and all business data will be permanently deleted
