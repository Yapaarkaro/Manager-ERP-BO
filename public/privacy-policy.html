Manager is a mobile-first ERP application for Indian small and medium businesses, developed and operated by Exanite Technologies Private Limited ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the Manager app ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Identity Information

  • Mobile phone number — used for OTP-based login and account identification
  • Full name — used for your business profile and invoices
  • Date of birth — collected during PAN-based verification only
  • GSTIN — used for GST-compliant business verification and invoice generation
  • PAN — used as an alternative to GSTIN for business identity verification

2.2 Business Information

  • Business name, legal name, and business type
  • Primary, branch, and warehouse addresses
  • Bank account details (account number, IFSC, bank name, account holder name, UPI ID)
  • Invoice preferences and numbering formats
  • Opening cash balance

2.3 Operational Data

  • Products (name, SKU, HSN code, price, tax rate, stock levels, barcode, images)
  • Customers and suppliers (names, phone numbers, GSTIN, addresses)
  • Invoices, payments, credit notes, and purchase orders
  • Returns, purchase invoices, and write-offs
  • Staff details (name, mobile, role, attendance, check-in/check-out records)
  • Chat messages and media shared within the App

2.4 Device & Technical Information

  • Device model, operating system, and version
  • Screen resolution, memory, and storage information
  • Network type, carrier information, and approximate IP address
  • App session and usage data

2.5 Location Information

Approximate and precise location, used for address selection, map-based features, and staff attendance geofencing.

2.6 Media & Files

  • Photos captured or uploaded for product catalogues
  • Voice notes and files shared in business chats
  • Staff profile images

4. Information Shared with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers who help operate the App:

4.1 Supabase (supabase.com)

Our primary backend infrastructure provider. All app data is stored and processed on Supabase's servers. Supabase acts as a data processor under our instructions.

4.2 IDfy (idfy.com)

Used for GSTIN and PAN verification during onboarding. We send your GSTIN or PAN number, legal name, and date of birth to IDfy solely for identity verification purposes.

4.3 AppyFlow (appyflow.in)

Used as an alternative GSTIN lookup service. We send your GSTIN number for business information retrieval.

4.4 Google Maps Platform (google.com)

Used for address search, geocoding, and map display. Location coordinates and address inputs are sent to Google's APIs for map-related features.

4.5 Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org)

When you scan a product barcode, the barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts to retrieve publicly available product information. No personal data is sent.

4.6 ui-avatars.com

Staff names are used to generate default placeholder avatar images when no profile photo is uploaded.

We require all third-party service providers to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Data is stored on Supabase's secured servers with row-level security policies
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
  • We implement access controls to limit data access to authorised personnel only
  • Bank account details are stored encrypted and are never shared with payment processors

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will delete your business data, transaction history, staff records, and associated files from our servers. Some data may be retained for up to 90 days in backups before permanent deletion. We may retain certain records where required by applicable Indian law (such as GST records under the CGST Act).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your business data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@getmanager.in.

8. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account within the App. This will permanently remove your account, business profile, transaction history, staff records, and all associated data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

You may also request account deletion by emailing support@getmanager.in or using the form at getmanager.in/account-deletion.

9. Children's Privacy

Manager is intended for use by business owners and professionals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

10. Location Data

The App requests location access for:

  • Selecting your business, branch, or warehouse address on a map
  • Staff attendance geofencing (checking in/out based on proximity to work location)

You can disable location access in your device settings at any time. This will limit map-based features.
